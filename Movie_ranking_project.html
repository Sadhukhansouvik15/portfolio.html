Money Heist

Money Heist (originally titled La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish heist crime drama series that follows a group of robbers who execute meticulously planned heists on the Royal Mint of Spain and the Bank of Spain, guided by their enigmatic leader, "The Professor." The series explores themes of resistance, loyalty, and love, interwoven with intense action and intricate plotting. Praised for its complex characters and gripping narrative, it has gained a massive international following and has become one of the most popular non-English language series on Netflix

Hridayam

Hridayam is a 2022 Indian Malayalam-language coming-of-age romantic drama film written and directed by Vineeth Sreenivasan. The movie stars Pranav Mohanlal, Kalyani Priyadarshan, and Darshana Rajendran. It follows the life journey of Arun Neelakandan, from his carefree college days to his experiences as an adult, exploring themes of love, friendship, and self-discovery. With a nostalgic narrative and a soulful soundtrack by Hesham Abdul Wahab, "Hridayam" received widespread acclaim for its heartfelt storytelling and performances.

Madgaon Express

Dodo, Ayush, and Pinku make an ordinary trio of boys who've been best friends since they were 10. Years have passed and lives have changed but one thing hasn't; their age-old dream of doing a Goa trip. They reunite years later and decide to take a trip to Goa, but what follows is a sequence of very bad decisions taken by three very luckless boys and unintentionally hilarious situations
